PatientTrack

What is PatientTrack?

PatientTrack is an app which is used to help people with Dementia, their carers, and their family members.

What does it do?

This app allows you to navigate home using Google Maps at the click of a button!
It also sends your location to our secure database so that you carer or family members can find you!

Fantastic! So how do I get started?

Simple! Just fill in the short registration form and proceed to login.
Then just click the Home button and you'll be shown directions immediately!
Once you're logged in you'll stay logged in (unless you log out) so you don't need to worry about remembering passwords.

What if I move house?

Don't worry! We've got you covered!
Your carer can change your home address from within their app!
